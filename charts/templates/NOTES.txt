Thank you for installing Observability in a Box (OBSB)!

To access OpenSearch Dashboards:
  kubectl port-forward svc/opensearch-dashboards 5601:5601 -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

To access Zabbix:
  kubectl port-forward svc/zabbix 8080:80 -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

Custom Stacks:
  Your stacks definitions have been loaded from values.yaml and rendered into:
    ConfigMap: obsb-stacks
  These generate:
    - Zabbix agents (DaemonSet or Sidecar)
    - Logshipper inputs
    - Future CRD/operator-based resources
